# Chorley Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** The Chorley and District Building Society (trading as Chorley Building Society)
- **Founded:** 7 March 1859, originally as The Chorley Permanent Benefit Building Society, to help local mill workers buy their homes
- **Headquarters:** Key House, Foxhole Road, Chorley, Lancashire, PR7 1NZ
- **Total assets:** £427.5 million (as at February 2025, up from £406m in 2024)
- **Savings balances (shares and deposits):** £367 million (as at February 2024)
- **Mortgage balances:** £312 million (as at February 2024)
- **Profit before tax:** £0.4 million (year ended February 2024)
- **Total income:** £7.2 million (year ended February 2024)
- **Members:** Approximately 28,000
- **Staff:** 79 employees (February 2025); approximately 60 full-time equivalent
- **CEO:** Stephen Penlington (Chief Executive)
- **Mutual status:** Independent mutual building society, owned by its members — no shareholders, not listed on the stock exchange. All profits go back into the Society to benefit members.
- **BSA member:** Yes, member of the Building Societies Association
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes — authorised by the Prudential Regulation Authority and regulated by both the FCA and PRA (FRN 206023)
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Tagline / positioning:** "Simple, flexible and of benefit to our Members" — the oldest building society in Lancashire
- **Identity:** Small, community-focused mutual; known for specialist mortgage products, particularly later life lending, and personal service with a common-sense approach to underwriting

## Products

### Savings

Chorley Building Society offers a broad range of personal and business savings accounts. The Society has a strong reputation for savings products alongside its mortgage range.

**Account types:**
- **Easy Access Accounts** — Deposit and withdraw freely with variable rates of interest
- **ISAs (Cash ISAs)** — Tax-free savings including Easy Access, Notice, and Fixed-Rate Cash ISAs
- **Notice Accounts** — Range of notice periods; unlimited deposits and withdrawals subject to notice
- **Regular Saver Account** — Save from £25–£400 each month; variable rate
- **Young Savers** — Range of accounts for young people including ISAs and regular savers
- **Over 60's Savings Account** — Tiered rate of interest; save from £1–£500,000 with unlimited access
- **Home Saver Accounts** — Save £100–£500 per month for up to five years; £500 cashback when taking out and completing on a Chorley BS mortgage
- **Regional Savings Accounts** — Additional product benefits for local members
- **Business Savings Accounts** — Earn interest on business savings; up to 4 people can manage the account; unlimited deposits up to £500,000 (launched recently)

**Smart Money People rating:** 4.86/5 based on 1,818 reviews (Excellent) — 87% five-star

### Mortgages (Specialist Lending)

Chorley Building Society is widely recognised for its **specialist and niche mortgage products** with a common-sense, manually underwritten approach. They lend across England and Wales with individual case assessment as a key differentiator. The Society has won multiple awards for specialist mortgage provision.

**Mortgage product range:**
- **First Time Buyer Mortgages** — Exclusive products up to 95% LTV with cashback incentive and no application or scheme fees
- **Fixed Rate Mortgages** — Purchases and remortgages up to 95% LTV
- **Discounted Mortgages** — Purchases and remortgages up to 95% LTV with no application fee
- **Buy to Let** — Wide variety including limited company BTL and first-time landlords (non-portfolio)
- **Later Life Lending** — For applicants aged 75+ at application and/or at end of term; capital repayment, interest only, or part & part basis. Won **Later Life Lender of the Year** at MoneyAge Awards 2023
- **Credit Renew** — For borrowers who have experienced a life event impacting their credit file
- **Shared Ownership** — Supporting the Government's Shared Ownership scheme with no application fee
- **Help to Buy** — Remortgage options available
- **First Homes** — Supporting the Government's First Homes scheme up to 95% LTV
- **Discount Market Scheme** — With cashback incentive for A or B EPC rating
- **Self Build** — Via authorised BuildLoan intermediaries only
- **Holiday Let** — Up to 80% LTV across England and Wales
- **Second Home** — Up to 85% LTV; for commuter homes, weekend retreats, or for dependents
- **Large Loans** — For larger borrowing requirements

**Key mortgage features:**
- **Common-sense, manually underwritten** approach — every case individually assessed with direct access to an underwriter
- Flexible lending across capital repayment, interest only, or part & part basis
- Competitive rates with no application fee on many products
- Strong intermediary/broker relationships — 8 consecutive 5-star ratings in Smart Money People Mortgage Broker Feedback survey
- Lending up to 95% LTV on standard residential products

### Intermediary Services

The Society has a well-developed intermediary channel with:
- **Broker Online Portal** for submitting Decisions in Principle, mortgage applications, and secure document uploads
- Dedicated Business Development Managers (BDMs)
- Direct access to underwriters for brokers
- Published lending criteria with common-sense flexibility

## Digital & Technology

**Current state:**
- **Chorley Online** — Web-based portal for savings account management. Allows members to view accounts, make transactions, and manage savings online.
- **No dedicated mobile app currently** — but this is changing with the TCS partnership

**Strategic digital transformation (announced December 2024):**
- Chorley Building Society entered a **strategic partnership with Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)** to transform its core banking system and front-end digital platforms
- **TCS BaNCS** for Core Banking — replacing legacy systems
- **TCS Digital Home Lending Solution** — for mortgage origination
- **New mobile app** — to be developed as part of the transformation
- The Society views this as strategically important to achieve ambitious growth plans while retaining personal approach
- CEO Stephen Penlington: "We are very proud of our mutual values and our Member-centric approach... It was important to us that we found a partner that could support our ambitious growth plans, whilst sharing our core values."
- Kimberley Roby (Customer Services Director) led the extensive selection process

**Known digital challenges:**
- The existing online platform (Chorley Online) receives criticism for being difficult to use, with some users reporting issues completing applications online
- No mobile app (yet) — a significant gap compared to larger societies
- Account opening and transfer processes can be slow compared to digital-first providers (e.g., postal verification)
- The TCS partnership is expected to address these issues but implementation timelines are not yet public

- **Slow response to interest rate changes** — some customers want faster pass-through of Bank of England rate changes

## Member Demographics

- **Core geography:** Lancashire, particularly Chorley and South Ribble area, though mortgages lend nationally across England and Wales
- **Savings members:** Mix of local Lancashire residents (who value branch access) and national online savers attracted by competitive rates
- **Mortgage borrowers:** National reach through intermediary channel; includes specialist segments — later life borrowers (75+), credit repair customers, holiday let investors, buy-to-let landlords
- **Age profile:** Likely skewing older given later life lending specialism and Over 60's savings account; younger members served through Young Savers
- **Typical member:** Values personal service and human interaction; appreciates being treated as an individual rather than a number; may have been with the Society for many years
