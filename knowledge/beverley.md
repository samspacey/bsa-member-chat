# Beverley Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Beverley Building Society
- **Founded:** 1866, one of the UK's longest-established societies
- **Headquarters:** Saturday Market, Beverley, East Riding of Yorkshire
- **Total assets:** £206 million (December 2023)
- **Staff:** 34 employees (2023)
- **CEO:** Janet Bedford
- **Chair:** Karen Wint
- **Net income:** £1.23 million (2023)
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its saving and borrowing members
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Tagline / positioning:** One of the UK's smallest and most traditional building societies — "Doing things differently"
- **Identity:** Very small, single-branch mutual focused on personal service, common-sense lending, and East Yorkshire community; among the smallest building societies in the UK
- **Former second branch:** Pocklington (1998–2009, closed to save ~£150,000/year)

## Branch Network

Beverley Building Society has just **1 branch**:

1. **Saturday Market, Beverley** — The Society's sole branch and headquarters in the East Riding of Yorkshire market town

Members can also manage accounts by phone and post. The Society does not rely on call centres or automated systems.

## Products

### Savings

Beverley Building Society offers straightforward, no-gimmick savings with a pledge of fair treatment for new and existing customers alike:

- **Instant Access Account** — From £1, variable rate (1.60%)
- **30 Day Notice Account** — From £1,000, variable (2.35%)
- **45 Day Notice Account** — From £5,000, variable (2.85%)
- **60 Day Notice Account** — From £5,000, variable (3.10%)
- **90 Day Notice Account Issue 2** — From £5,000, variable (3.60%)
- **Fixed Rate Bond Issue 14** — From £5,000, 2-year fixed (3.90%)
- **Cash ISA** — From £1, variable (2.40%/2.50%)
- **Junior Cash ISA** — From £1, variable (3.85%)
- **Monthly Savings Account** — From £10, variable (2.70% / 3.20% with conditional bonus)
- **Regular Saver Issue 1** — From £10, £10–£150/month, 5.50% including 4.50% bonus
- **Young Savers Account** — From £1 up to £25,000 (3.00%)
- **Postal Account** — From £1,000, variable (1.60%/1.70%)
- **Business Online Account** — From £1,000, variable (1.45%)
- **Postal Business Account** — From £1,000, variable (1.45%)

**Key features:**
- "No gimmicks, no surprises" pledge
- Never offers better rates to new customers than existing loyal savers
- Monthly interest available on some accounts over £5,000
- No pushy sales tactics

### Mortgages

Beverley Building Society specialises in niche mortgages and common-sense lending:

- **Standard residential mortgages**
- **Self-employed borrowers**
- **Contractor mortgages**
- **Self-build mortgages**
- **Unusual property purchases**
- **Later life lending**
- **Family support mortgages** — Helping families get each other onto the property ladder

**Key features:**
- **Manual underwriting** — Not credit scoring; common-sense approach based on full understanding of circumstances
- **5-star Smart Money People rating** — Evidencing reputation for going the extra mile
- Individual assessment of each case
- Lends UK-wide for mortgage products
- "We don't try to fit everyone into boxes"

## Key Differentiators

1. **One of the UK's smallest building societies** — Very personal, intimate service
2. **Single-branch mutual** — All service from one location in Beverley, East Riding of Yorkshire
3. **Manual underwriting** — Common-sense lending decisions, not credit scores or algorithms
4. **Niche mortgage specialist** — Self-employed, contractors, self-build, unusual properties, later life, family support
5. **"No gimmicks" savings pledge** — Fair treatment; no better rates for new vs existing customers
6. **No bonuses or sales targets** — No pressure on staff to sell; genuine advice
7. **People-centred philosophy** — Actively choosing personal service over digital automation
8. **Strong local identity** — Deeply rooted in Beverley and East Yorkshire since 1866
9. **5-star Smart Money People rating** — Evidencing consistent customer satisfaction
10. **Staff know customers by name** — First-name-terms relationships with members

## Member Demographics

- Strong East Yorkshire base, centred on Beverley market town

- Members who value personal, face-to-face relationship with their building society
- Self-employed borrowers, contractors, and those with non-standard incomes
- Older borrowers using later-life lending products
- Families using family-support mortgages
- Local business customers through business savings accounts
- Young savers through Junior ISA and Young Savers accounts
- Members who actively choose a small, traditional building society over larger institutions
