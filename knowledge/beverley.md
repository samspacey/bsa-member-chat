# Beverley Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Beverley Building Society
- **Founded:** 1866, one of the UK's longest-established societies
- **Headquarters:** Saturday Market, Beverley, East Riding of Yorkshire
- **Total assets:** £206 million (December 2023)
- **Staff:** 34 employees (2023)
- **CEO:** Janet Bedford
- **Chair:** Karen Wint
- **Net income:** £1.23 million (2023)
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its saving and borrowing members
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Tagline / positioning:** One of the UK's smallest and most traditional building societies — "Doing things differently"
- **Identity:** Very small, single-branch mutual focused on personal service, common-sense lending, and East Yorkshire community; among the smallest building societies in the UK
- **Former second branch:** Pocklington (1998–2009, closed to save ~£150,000/year)

## Branch Network

Beverley Building Society has just **1 branch**:

1. **Saturday Market, Beverley** — The Society's sole branch and headquarters in the East Riding of Yorkshire market town

Members can also manage accounts by phone and post. The Society does not rely on call centres or automated systems.
