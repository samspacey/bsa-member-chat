# Leek United Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Leek United Building Society (trading as Leek Building Society)
- **Founded:** 1 January 1863 (as Leek United Permanent Benefit Building Society)
- **Headquarters:** Leek, Staffordshire, England
- **Total assets:** £1,313.9 million (as at 31 December 2023, up 6.1% on 2022)
- **Staff:** 182 employees (2023)
- **CEO:** Andy Deeks (appointed June 2024)
- **Mutual status:** Independent mutual building society
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA regulated:** Yes (reference 100014)
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Ranking:** 17th largest building society in the UK by total assets
- **Original motto:** "Firm and Lasting (as the pyramids)"
- **Trading name:** Leek Building Society (note: distinct from "Leek Building Society" in societies list — same entity, different legal vs trading names)
- **Tagline / positioning:** "A force for good" — independent community-focused mutual serving Staffordshire, Cheshire and Derbyshire
- **Smart Money People rating:** 4.90/5 (excellent)
- **Awards:** UK's Best Specialist Mortgage Provider — 2025 British Bank Awards

## Products

### Savings

A broad range of savings accounts:

- **Instant Access** — Flexible deposits and withdrawals
- **Regular Savers** — Monthly savings plans
- **Fixed Rate Bonds** — Lump sum savings at guaranteed rates
- **Cash ISAs** — Tax-free savings with various access options
- **Notice Accounts** — Higher rates in exchange for withdrawal notice
- **Children's Savings** — Accounts for young savers

### Mortgages

Known as a **specialist mortgage provider** (award-winning):

- **Residential Mortgages** — Standard home purchase
- **First Time Buyers** — Tailored products for new buyers
- **Remortgaging** — Switch existing mortgage
- **Buy-to-Let** — Landlord lending
- **Self-Build** — Construction finance
- **Specialist/Complex Cases** — Individual underwriting for non-standard situations
- Available direct and through intermediaries/brokers

## Strengths

- Award-winning specialist mortgage provider (2025 British Bank Awards)
- Excellent customer ratings (4.90/5 on Smart Money People)
- Strong regional presence with 12 branches
- Over 160 years of continuous operation
- Individual underwriting for complex mortgage cases
- Independent mutual — member-owned

## Weaknesses

- Regional focus limited to Staffordshire, Cheshire, and Derbyshire
- Relatively small national profile despite strong local reputation
- Limited digital/app offering compared to larger societies
- Confusing naming: legal name "Leek United" vs trading name "Leek Building Society" (and a separate "Leek" entry in BSA data)

## Member Demographics

- Residents of Staffordshire, Cheshire, and Derbyshire primarily
- Specialist mortgage borrowers (complex cases, self-build)
- Savers seeking personal service and competitive rates
- Local community organisations benefiting from Charitable Foundation
- Broker-referred borrowers for specialist products
