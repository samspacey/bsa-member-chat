# Stafford Railway Building Society (The Stafford Building Society) - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** The Stafford Building Society (rebranded 1 March 2024)
- **Former names:** Stafford Railway Permanent Benefit Building Society (1877); Stafford Railway Building Society (1957–2024)
- **Founded:** 1877, by a group of railway workers from the London & North Western Railway Company in Stafford
- **Headquarters:** Stafford, Staffordshire
- **Total assets:** £324 million (October 2024)
- **Net income:** £6.19 million (October 2024)
- **Members:** ~15,000+
- **Staff:** 50+ employees
- **CEO:** Steven Jones
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its saving and borrowing members
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Website:** srbs.co.uk (retaining the SRBS domain)
- **Tagline / positioning:** "Financially Fair. Community Strong. Since 1877" — Stafford's oldest mutual
- **Rebrand:** Changed name from Stafford Railway Building Society to The Stafford Building Society in March 2024 to better reflect the wider communities now served
- **Identity:** Staffordshire-rooted mutual with railway heritage; committed to fair financial products, community support, and member-owned values

## Branch Network

The Stafford Building Society has a **single main branch** in Stafford:

1. **Stafford Branch** — Open Monday to Saturday; staffed by a local branch team

Members can also manage accounts by phone, post, and online.

## Products

### Savings

The Stafford Building Society provides savings products including:

- **Passbook Savings** — Traditional passbook-based accounts still actively maintained
- **Junior ISAs** — For young savers
- **Long-term Savings Accounts**
- **General savings range** — Various account types for different needs
- **Affinity Accounts** — Savings products that help raise money for local causes

**Key features:**
- Branch-based with personal service
- Passbook savings still supported
- Affinity accounts link savings to community giving
- Available in branch and through other channels

### Mortgages

The Stafford Building Society provides mortgage lending with a personal approach:

- **Residential mortgages** — Helping people buy homes
- **Available through branch and intermediaries**

**Key features:**
- Individual, personal approach to lending
- Rooted in Staffordshire community
- Available across wider area through intermediaries
