# Scottish Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Scottish Building Society
- **Former name:** Edinburgh Friendly Property Investment Company (1848–1929)
- **Founded:** 1848, making it the oldest remaining building society in the world
- **Headquarters:** SBS House, 193 Dalry Road, Edinburgh, EH11 2EF (Head Office); main customer-facing Relationship Centre at 20 Frederick Street, Edinburgh
- **Total assets:** £866.5 million (as at January 2025, up from £740.7m in January 2024 — doubled in 5 years)
- **Members:** ~33,600 (January 2024)
- **Staff:** ~102 employees
- **CEO:** Paul Denton (joined 2019 with new leadership team; received Lifetime Achievement award at 2024 Scottish Mortgage Awards)
- **Profit before tax:** £4.0 million (year ended January 2024)
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its saving and borrowing members
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Tagline / positioning:** "Powered by purpose" — Scotland's only independent building society, the world's oldest remaining building society
- **Identity:** Scotland-focused mutual serving local communities with simple, straightforward products; strong commitment to branch presence and passbook savings alongside digital tools

## Products

### Savings

Scottish Building Society offers a straightforward range of savings products with "no teaser rates" — the same rate for new and existing customers. Products available both in branch (with passbooks) and online.

**Account types:**
- **Instant Access Saver** — Branch-based, from £1, variable rate (2.25% gross/AER)
- **Online Saver** — Online only, from £100, variable rate (2.25% gross/AER)
- **Saver Plus** — Branch-based, from £100, higher rate (3.25%) with max 4 withdrawals per year
- **E-Saver Plus** — Online version of Saver Plus, same terms (3.25%)
- **Regular Saver** — Branch-based, from £50, up to £250/month for 12 months at 6.00% variable
- **Cash ISA** — Branch-based, from £100, variable rate (2.35% tax-free)
- **E-ISA** — Online version, same rate
- **Fixed Rate ISAs** — 1-year and 2-year terms at 3.80% tax-free fixed
- **Members Fixed Rate ISA** — Loyalty product for existing members (12+ months), 3.80%
- **Savings Bonds** — 1-year and 2-year fixed rate bonds at 3.80%
- **E-Savings Bonds** — Online fixed bonds at 3.80%
- **Member Savings Bonds** — Loyalty bonds for existing members (12+ months), 3.80%
- **Junior Saver** — For under-18s
- **Children's accounts** available

**Key features:**
- No teaser rates — simple, transparent pricing
- Passbook savings still available in branches
- Member loyalty bonds offering enhanced rates
- Maximum balance up to £1,000,000 on most accounts

### Mortgages

Scottish Building Society lends primarily for properties in Scotland. Personal, individual underwriting approach.

**Mortgage product range:**
- **Residential Mortgages** — Up to 95% LTV, loans from £30,000 to £1,000,000
- **Remortgage** — Free legals and valuation (or £250 cashback)
- **Professional Mortgages** — For qualified professionals, loans from £50,000 to £1,000,000, up to 95% LTV, age 21+
- **Guarantor Mortgages** — For buyers who need family support, including students; loans £50,000–£300,000, up to 90% LTV
- **Retirement Interest Only (RIO)** — For applicants aged 55+, no maximum age, capital repaid on sale of property

**Key features:**
- Scotland-focused lending
- Individual, personal underwriting approach
- Guarantor mortgages available (including for students)
- No maximum age on retirement mortgages
