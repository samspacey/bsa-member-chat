# Tipton & Coseley Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Tipton & Coseley Building Society (trading as "The Tipton")
- **Founded:** 1 May 1901
- **Headquarters:** Owen Street, Tipton, West Midlands
- **Total assets:** £603.5 million (December 2024)
- **Savings balances:** £524 million (2024, record; up from £479m in 2023)
- **Gross mortgage lending:** £120 million (2024, record for second consecutive year; up from £116m in 2023)
- **Profit before tax:** £2.3 million (2024, down from £4.3m in 2023 due to tighter margins and increased investment)
- **Staff:** 120 employees (2024, up from previous years after 19 new recruits)
- **CEO:** Adam Evetts
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its saving and borrowing members
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Tagline / positioning:** "Serving our community since 1901" — tailored mortgage and savings products with a personal touch
- **Identity:** Black Country mutual with strong community roots; innovative lender expanding into new markets; investing in branches and technology

## Branch Network

Tipton & Coseley Building Society has **5 branches** across the Black Country and South Staffordshire:

1. **Tipton** — Owen Street, Tipton town centre (headquarters)
2. **Coseley**
3. **Bilston**
4. **Sedgley**
5. **Codsall** — Opened September 2025 (newest branch, expanding into South Staffordshire)

Members can also manage accounts by post, telephone, and digitally.

## Products

### Savings

The Tipton offers a variety of savings products:

- **Fixed rate savings accounts** — Various terms
- **Variable rate savings accounts** — Multiple options
- **Cash ISAs** — Tax-free savings
- **Children's accounts** — For young savers
- **Business savings** — For corporate customers

**Key features:**
- Savings rates 1.45% above market average (2024)
- Multiple channels: branch, postal, telephone, and digital app
- Retail savings balances reached record £524m in 2024
- Retail deposits grew £42m year-on-year

### Mortgages

The Tipton is an innovative mortgage lender, actively expanding into new market segments:

- **Standard residential mortgages**
- **Shared ownership mortgages** — Increased focus in 2024
- **Expat residential mortgages** — New market entered in 2024
- **Buy-to-let mortgages**
- **Specialist lending** — Various niche segments

**Key features:**
- Record gross lending of £120m in 2024 (second consecutive record year)
- 13% increase in mortgage retention rate for existing borrowers
- Innovative products in shared ownership and expat markets
- Individual assessment approach
- Available through branches and intermediaries
