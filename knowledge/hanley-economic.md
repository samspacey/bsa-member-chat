# Hanley Economic Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Hanley Economic Building Society (trading as "The Hanley")
- **Founded:** 1854 (originally as The Staffordshire Potteries Economic Permanent Benefit Building Society)
- **Founder:** Earl Granville
- **Headquarters:** Granville House, Festival Park, Hanley, Stoke-on-Trent, ST1 5TB
- **Total assets:** ~£528 million (2024 estimate, from £447 million in 2019)
- **Mortgage balances:** ~£380 million
- **Retail savings:** ~£480 million
- **Staff:** ~80
- **CEO:** Mark Selby
- **Net income:** £1.0 million (2019); Profit before tax ~£2.5 million (more recent)
- **Gross mortgage lending:** ~£95 million per year
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its members
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA regulated:** Yes (Registration Number 206024)
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Motto:** "Save Safely, Build Surely"
- **Tagline / positioning:** Helping members save for their futures and own their own homes since 1854
- **Identity:** A community-focused building society deeply rooted in the Staffordshire Potteries, with a strong emphasis on personal service, community involvement, and innovative local mortgage products
- **Subsidiaries:** Hanley Financial Services Ltd, Hanley Mortgage Services Ltd
- **Coat of Arms:** Granted 13 March 1972, featuring squirrel supporters and a motto "Save Safely Build Surely"

## Branch Network

Hanley Economic Building Society is consolidating its branch network:

- The Society has historically operated from its **Granville House** headquarters at Festival Park, Hanley, plus branch locations
- **Hanley branch on Lamb Street** is closing at 12pm on Saturday 7th March 2026, as part of plans to consolidate the branch network
- The Society serves members through branches, telephone, and online channels

## Products

### Savings

The Hanley offers a range of savings products, though the website provides limited detail on specific accounts:

**Account types:**
- Standard savings accounts
- **Jet Set Saver** — A unique promotional account launched in partnership with Connect Travel Stoke-on-Trent, giving members the chance to win a £10,000 holiday voucher and enjoy exclusive travel benefits

### Mortgages

The Hanley provides mortgage products with a focus on the local market:

**Mortgage types:**
- Standard residential mortgages
- **100% Local Rent to Own Mortgage** — Launched in May 2025, an innovative product allowing local residents to rent a property with a pathway to ownership without requiring a traditional deposit. This is a pioneering product in the building society sector.
- Specialist lending through intermediary channel

**Key features:**
- Individual case assessment
- Focus on the local Staffordshire market
- Available through direct and intermediary channels

## Community & Values

Hanley Economic places community involvement at the core of its identity:

### Mission and Values
- **"We put Members first"** — Delivering superior customer service for lifelong loyalty
- **"We do the right thing"** — Acting with integrity and being honest and principled
- **"We support our community"** — Devoting time to make a difference locally
- **"We take personal accountability"** — Working with passion and commitment
- **"We not me"** — Collaborative approach, sharing ideas and celebrating success

### Community Partnerships and Charities
- **Alice Charity** — Committed to the Fortunate 500 scheme
- **Glow** — Supporting adults and young people tackling domestic abuse
- **Dougie Mac Hospice** — Proud corporate fundraiser for the local hospice
- **YMCA** — Supporting the North Staffordshire Youth Campus
- **Hanley at Home Fund** — Supporting charities that help people manage and maintain their own homes
- **Connect Travel Stoke-on-Trent** — Partnership for the Jet Set Saver promotional account

### Volunteering
- Achieved **173 volunteering days in 2024** and aims to exceed that in 2025
- Staff actively encouraged to participate in community activities

## Key Differentiators

1. **Deep Potteries heritage** — Founded in 1854 in the heart of the Staffordshire Potteries, with a coat of arms granted in 1972
2. **100% Rent to Own Mortgage** — Innovative local mortgage product launched in 2025, allowing homeownership without a traditional deposit
3. **Strong community commitment** — Multiple charity partnerships and 173+ volunteering days per year
4. **Values-driven culture** — Clear mission and values framework ("We not me") embedded in operations
5. **Local focus** — Primarily serves the Stoke-on-Trent and Staffordshire community
6. **Mutual ownership** — Member-owned with profits reinvested for member benefit
7. **Creative product innovation** — Jet Set Saver with travel partnership shows willingness to create distinctive products

## Strengths

- Long heritage (170+ years) deeply embedded in the local community
- Innovative mortgage products (100% Rent to Own)
- Strong community volunteering culture
- Multiple charity partnerships across different causes
- Personal, member-first service approach
- Creative marketing and product partnerships (Jet Set Saver)

## Weaknesses

- Small asset base (~£528m) limits product range and competitive positioning
- Branch network being consolidated (Lamb Street Hanley closing March 2026)
- Limited geographic reach — primarily Stoke-on-Trent and surrounding area
- Small staff (~80) may limit capacity for growth
- Limited online/digital capabilities compared to larger societies
- Low public profile outside the Staffordshire region

## Member Demographics

- Primarily serves the **Stoke-on-Trent and Staffordshire** community
- Mix of local savers and mortgage borrowers
- The 100% Rent to Own mortgage targets **local residents** who aspire to homeownership but lack traditional deposits
- Community-minded members who value local connection and personal service
- Likely includes significant number of members with ties to the Potteries area's working heritage
