# Melton Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Melton Mowbray Building Society (trading as "The Melton")
- **Founded:** 1875
- **Headquarters:** Melton Mowbray, Leicestershire, East Midlands
- **Total assets:** ~£769 million (2024, up from £714 million in 2022)
- **Members:** Over 40,000
- **Staff:** ~141
- **CEO:** Simon Taylor
- **Net income:** £13.2 million (2022)
- **Mutual status:** Independent mutual, owned by its members
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Tagline / positioning:** Helping people buy homes and make the most of their savings since 1875
- **Identity:** A modern mutual building society firmly rooted in the rural market towns of the East Midlands, offering mortgages, savings, insurance, investment products, and independent financial advice through a compact branch network
- **Subsidiaries:** MBS Lending Ltd, Nexa Finance Ltd, MMBS Services Ltd
- **Website:** www.themelton.co.uk

## Branch Network

The Melton operates **5 branches** across the East Midlands:

1. **Melton Mowbray** — Principal office and main branch (Mon-Fri 9am-5pm, Sat 9am-12pm)
2. **Grantham** — Branch in the Lincolnshire market town (Mon-Fri 9am-5pm, Sat 9am-12pm)
3. **Oakham** — Branch in the Rutland county town
4. **Stamford** — Branch in the south Lincolnshire town
5. **Bourne** — Branch in the Lincolnshire market town

The branch network covers Leicestershire, Rutland, and Lincolnshire — primarily serving rural market towns.

## Community & Values

### Commitment to Mutuality
- Member-owned with no shareholders
- "Don't have to pay shareholders dividends — so we can share the results of our success with our members through higher interest rates for savers, lower rates for borrowers and providing better services"
- Profits reinvested for member benefit

### Community Support
- **Local charity support** — Financial and voluntary support for local charities
- Staff contribute "time, experience and knowledge" as well as financial support
- Roots and heritage firmly in the Melton Mowbray area

### Customer Service
- Customer advisors available Mon-Fri 9am-5pm and Saturday 9am-12pm in Melton Mowbray and Grantham branches
- Emphasis on community-based, personal service

## Key Differentiators

1. **Independent financial advice** — Offering independent personal financial advice is unusual for a building society of this size
2. **Rural market town focus** — Serving Melton Mowbray, Oakham, Grantham, Stamford, and Bourne — primarily rural and semi-rural East Midlands communities
3. **Multiple subsidiaries** — MBS Lending Ltd, Nexa Finance Ltd suggest diversified business model beyond traditional building society operations
4. **Significant scale for a local society** — £769 million in assets and 40,000+ members is substantial for a 5-branch operation
5. **Strong mutual values** — Clear articulation of how mutuality benefits members through better rates and service
6. **Heritage since 1875** — Nearly 150 years of service in the Melton Mowbray area

## Strengths

- Growing asset base (£769m, up from £714m in 2022)
- Strong membership base (40,000+)
- Independent financial advice offering broadens value proposition
- Diverse subsidiary structure suggesting business sophistication
- Good branch coverage across rural East Midlands market towns
- Clear mutual values articulation
- Personal, face-to-face service in branch
- Multiple distribution channels (branch, phone, online, intermediary)

## Weaknesses

- Geographically concentrated in rural East Midlands (Leicestershire, Rutland, Lincolnshire)
- Only 5 branches — limited physical footprint
- Rural market town locations may limit growth potential
- Competing with both larger societies and other local Midlands societies
- Digital capabilities appear modest compared to larger competitors
- Limited national profile

## Member Demographics

- Over **40,000 members**
- Primarily serves **Leicestershire, Rutland, and Lincolnshire** communities
- Rural and semi-rural residents of East Midlands market towns
- Members who value personal service and local branch access
- Savers looking for competitive rates from a mutual provider
- First-time buyers and home movers in the rural East Midlands
- Members seeking independent financial advice
- Likely includes significant agricultural and rural business community members given the market town locations (Melton Mowbray is known as the "Rural Capital of Food")
