# Harpenden Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Harpenden Building Society
- **Founded:** 3 March 1953, by a group of local business people in Harpenden, Hertfordshire
- **Headquarters:** Harpenden, Hertfordshire
- **Total assets:** ~£339 million (December 2023)
- **Retail deposits:** £306 million (2023)
- **Mortgages:** £275 million (2023)
- **Profit after tax:** £0.89 million (2023)
- **Members:** Not publicly stated; 1,701 voted at 2025 AGM
- **Staff:** Over 80 employees
- **CEO:** Tracie Pearce (appointed May 2024; joins from Santander where she was Chief Customer Officer, Homes; over 29 years' experience across Nationwide, HSBC, and Santander)
- **Former CEOs:** Richard Doe (2022–2024), Richard Steed (prior)
- **Chair:** Stephen Richardson
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its saving and borrowing members
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes (firm reference number 157260)
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Tagline / positioning:** "We believe we are different" — helping people towards a safe and secure financial future
- **Awards:** Won What Mortgage Award for Best Local Building Society in 2015, 2016, and every year since 2017; named UK's Best Local Building Society in 2024
- **Cultural reference:** Mentioned in Monty Python's Big Red Book (1971) in The Poems of Ewen McTeagle
- **Identity:** Small, award-winning mutual in Hertfordshire; known for individual mortgage underwriting and strong community engagement

### Mortgages

Harpenden Building Society provides individually underwritten mortgages:

**Mortgage types:**
- **Residential Mortgages** — Various criteria
- **Buy-to-Let Mortgages** — Specific BTL criteria
- **Joint Borrower Sole Proprietor (JBSP)** — Family support mortgages

**Key features:**
- **Individually underwritten** — "We get to completely understand our customer's specific needs"
- Available direct and through intermediaries
- Dedicated intermediary section with criteria, affordability calculator, and case submission portal
- Decision in Principle available
- Valuation fee information and tariff of charges published

### Intermediary Channel

- Dedicated intermediary mortgage range
- Affordability calculator for brokers
- Separate criteria documents for residential and BTL
- Online case registration and submission
- Active broker relationship programme with feedback mechanism
