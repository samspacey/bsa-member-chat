# Cambridge Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** The Cambridge Building Society
- **Formerly:** Cambridgeshire Permanent Benefit Building Society (until 1945)
- **Founded:** 1850 (176 years old)
- **Headquarters:** Cambridge, Cambridgeshire, England
- **Total assets:** Just over £2 billion (2024, up 6.1% — surpassing £2 billion for the first time in the society's 175-year history)
- **Reserves:** £134.1 million (2024)
- **Profit before tax:** £11.4 million (2024, down from £20.5m in 2023 — primarily due to interest rate environment)
- **Gross mortgage lending (2024):** £305 million
- **Revenue:** £37.6 million (2022)
- **Total equity:** £113.9 million (2022)
- **Staff:** ~236 (2022)
- **CEO:** Peter Burrows (Chief Executive)
- **CFO:** Richard Brockbank (Chief Financial Officer)
- **Chair:** John Spence
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its members (customers)
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA regulated:** Yes
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Ranking:** 13th largest building society in the UK
- **Tagline / positioning:** "Whatever you're saving for, we'll be here"
- **Identity:** Community-focused building society with strong Cambridge roots; award-winning support for first-time buyers; significant community fund

## Products

### Savings

The Cambridge Building Society offers a range of savings products including:

**Account types:**
- **Easy Access Savings** — Variable rate, instant access
- **ISAs** — Tax-free savings options
- **Fixed-Term Bonds** — Lock money away for higher rates
- **100-Day Notice Account** — Popular product offering a rate boost without long-term commitment (noted as particularly popular with members)
- **Children's savings** — Junior accounts

**Key features:**
- About 10% savings ratio among members (2024)
- Strong savings inflows — competitive rates in the market
- Savings products available in-store and online

### Mortgages

The Cambridge Building Society offers a comprehensive mortgage range with a strong focus on first-time buyers.

**Mortgage types:**
- **First-time buyer mortgages** — Core strength; won **Gold for Best First Time Buyer Mortgage Provider** for three consecutive years
- **Home mover mortgages** — For those moving home
- **Remortgages** — Switching from another provider
- **Retirement Interest Only (RIO) Mortgages** — Helping older borrowers stay in their home, free up cash for family, or fund home improvements
- **Variable and fixed rate options** — Range of terms and rates

**Key mortgage features:**
- Lends across all of England and Wales (expanded from regional focus in March 2020)
- About 1 in 5 mortgages goes to first-time buyers
- Friendly mortgage advisers available in-store and by phone
- Customer sentiment around fixed vs variable shifting as interest rates evolve
- Core Capital Deferred Shares issued (second building society after Nationwide to do so, 2017 — £15m partnership with Cambridgeshire County Council Pension Fund)

## Community & Values

- **The Cambridge Building Society Community Fund** — Provides grants of up to £20,000 to local charities and community groups that find new ways to help people have a home or improve access to shelter and housing
- **Community-focused** — "Seeking to make a profit that keeps the balance sheet healthy and positions us well to keep lending and doing all the good community work"
- **Not profit-maximising** — As a mutual, the society explicitly states it doesn't seek to maximise profit; it aims for a level that maintains financial health and supports community work
- **First-time buyer champion** — Strong focus on helping people onto the property ladder
- **Local investment** — Branch openings and refurbishments demonstrate commitment to physical presence
- **Long heritage:** 176 years of continuous operation in Cambridge

- Limited information publicly available about specific savings rates/products (website rendering issues)
