# Dudley Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Dudley Building Society
- **Founded:** 1858, over 165 years of lending history
- **Headquarters:** Brierley Hill, West Midlands (not Dudley town itself)
- **Total assets:** £636 million (March 2024)
- **Members' deposits:** £560 million (March 2024)
- **Customer loans:** £477 million (March 2024)
- **Members:** ~30,918 (2014 figure; likely grown since)
- **Staff:** ~91 employees
- **CEO:** James Paterson
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its saving and borrowing members
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Smart Money People rating:** 4.84/5 (Excellent) with 97% overall feedback rating
- **Tagline / positioning:** "There to help people live better lives" — specialist lender with impeccable customer experience
- **Identity:** West Midlands community mutual; specialist/niche mortgage lender with manual underwriting; strong community focus

## Branch Network

Dudley Building Society has **6 branches** across the West Midlands:

1. **Brierley Hill** (Head Office)
2. **Dudley**
3. **Halesowen**
4. **Stourbridge**
5. **Kingswinford**
6. **Shifnal** — Pop-up bank opened September 2025, bucking the trend of financial service closures in market towns

Members can also manage accounts online, by phone, and by post.

## Products

### Savings

Dudley Building Society provides personal and business savings:

- **Personal Savings Accounts** — Including instant access, notice accounts, fixed rate bonds
- **Cash ISAs** — Tax-free savings options
- **Children's Savings** — Including the Squirrel Saver account for young savers (with passbook)
- **Business Savings Accounts**

**Key features:**
- Interest calculated daily on account balance
- Most accounts pay interest on 31 March (some semi-annually or at maturity/anniversary)
- Monthly interest available on some accounts
- ISA interest is tax-free
- All accounts paid gross (no tax deduction at source)

### Mortgages

Dudley Building Society is a **specialist lender** known for flexible, individually assessed mortgage decisions:

**Key approach:**
- "No two borrowers are the same" — individual manual underwriting
- Focus on underserved markets
- Specialist lending in areas competitors don't serve
- Achieve returns to cover cost base while adding to capital

**Products include:**
- Standard residential mortgages
- Later-life/retirement lending
- Specialist/niche mortgage products
- Remortgages

**Key features:**
- Individual case assessment
- Manual underwriting — not algorithm-driven
- Focus on areas underserved by competitors
- Available through branches and intermediaries
