# Vernon Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Vernon Building Society
- **Founded:** 1924 in Stockport, Cheshire — celebrated centenary in 2024
- **Headquarters:** Stockport, Greater Manchester
- **Total assets:** £506 million (December 2024, exceeding £500m for first time; up 10% year-on-year)
- **Savings balances:** £447 million (2024, up 9%)
- **Gross mortgage lending:** £89.6 million (2024, up from £76.4m in 2023)
- **Profit before tax:** £3.5 million (2024)
- **CEO:** Darren Ditchburn (appointed December 2024, replacing Steve Fletcher who retired after leading the Society; previously from Leek Building Society)
- **Former CEO:** Steve Fletcher (retired December 2024)
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its saving and borrowing members, not shareholders
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes (registration number 195475)
- **FSCS protected:** Yes (eligible deposits protected up to £120,000 per person; £240,000 for joint accounts)
- **Tagline / positioning:** Described as Stockport's "best kept secret" — a trusted local building society helping members grow savings and buy homes
- **Named after:** George John Warren (Lord Vernon), who donated land for Vernon Park in Stockport
- **Identity:** Community-focused Greater Manchester mutual; competitive rates, face-to-face service, expanding branch network

## Branch Network

Vernon Building Society has **7 branches** across Greater Manchester and Cheshire, all recently refurbished as part of a £1.2m investment programme completed in 2024:

1. **Stockport** (town centre)
2. **Edgeley**
3. **Marple**
4. **Bramhall**
5. **Hazel Grove**
6. **Poynton**
7. **Reddish**

**Expansion plans:** The Vernon is bucking the trend of branch closures, with plans to invest in new community branches across Greater Manchester. The ambition is to have a presence in all 10 Greater Manchester boroughs by 2030.

**Marketing:** Launched first TV advertising campaign to increase visibility across Greater Manchester and Cheshire.

## Key Differentiators

1. **Centenary mutual** — 100 years of continuous service since 1924
2. **Counter-trend branch expansion** — Investing in new branches across Greater Manchester while competitors close theirs; ambitious target of all 10 boroughs by 2030
3. **£1.2m branch refurbishment** — All branches modernised into community-focused spaces
4. **Rapid growth** — Assets exceeded £500m for first time in 2024, growing 10% year-on-year
5. **Underserved market focus** — Active commitment to helping people who struggle to get mortgages from mainstream lenders
6. **Dual charitable giving** — Both Vernon Charitable Foundation (larger grants) and Community Stars (grassroots support)
7. **Deep Stockport/Greater Manchester roots** — Named after a local benefactor; deeply embedded in Greater Manchester identity
8. **First TV campaign** — Actively building brand awareness beyond traditional catchment area
9. **New leadership** — Fresh CEO Darren Ditchburn (from Leek BS) bringing external perspective while maintaining community focus
10. **Member value** — Profits reinvested in Society and community rather than distributed to shareholders

## Member Demographics

- Predominantly Greater Manchester and Cheshire-based savers and borrowers
- Strong local community ties across Stockport and surrounding areas
- Members who value face-to-face branch service in their local high street
- Growing reach across wider Greater Manchester as branch network expands
- Mortgage borrowers include those underserved by mainstream lenders
- Long-standing member relationships — some spanning decades given the Society's 100-year history
