# Buckinghamshire Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Buckinghamshire Building Society
- **Former name:** Chalfont & District Permanent Building Society (1907–later renamed)
- **Founded:** 1907, by a group of local businessmen in Chalfont St Giles, Buckinghamshire
- **Headquarters:** Chalfont St Giles, Buckinghamshire
- **Total assets:** £361 million (December 2024; down from £381m in 2023)
- **Members:** ~11,400 (2022)
- **Profit before tax:** £0.9 million (2024; down from £1.3m in 2023)
- **CEO:** Dan Wass (joined September 2022)
- **Chair:** Dick Jenkins
- **Staff:** Approximately 40-50 employees
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its saving and borrowing members
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Website:** www.bucksbs.co.uk
- **Online banking:** mybbs portal
- **Tagline / positioning:** "Helping people achieve financial security, providing a safe home for savings and enabling home ownership"
- **Identity:** Small, single-branch Buckinghamshire mutual aspiring to be the best small building society; deeply embedded in the Chalfont community with strong local heritage
