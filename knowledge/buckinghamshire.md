# Buckinghamshire Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Buckinghamshire Building Society
- **Former name:** Chalfont & District Permanent Building Society (1907–later renamed)
- **Founded:** 1907, by a group of local businessmen in Chalfont St Giles, Buckinghamshire
- **Headquarters:** Chalfont St Giles, Buckinghamshire
- **Total assets:** £361 million (December 2024; down from £381m in 2023)
- **Members:** ~11,400 (2022)
- **Profit before tax:** £0.9 million (2024; down from £1.3m in 2023)
- **CEO:** Dan Wass (joined September 2022)
- **Chair:** Dick Jenkins
- **Staff:** Approximately 40-50 employees
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its saving and borrowing members
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Website:** www.bucksbs.co.uk
- **Online banking:** mybbs portal
- **Tagline / positioning:** "Helping people achieve financial security, providing a safe home for savings and enabling home ownership"
- **Identity:** Small, single-branch Buckinghamshire mutual aspiring to be the best small building society; deeply embedded in the Chalfont community with strong local heritage

## Branch Network

Buckinghamshire Building Society has **1 branch**:

1. **Chalfont St Giles** — The Society's sole branch and headquarters

Phone: 01494 879500. Online banking available through mybbs portal.

## Products

### Savings

Buckinghamshire Building Society provides a range of savings products:

- **Savings accounts** available through the branch and mybbs online portal
- Product range includes standard savings categories
- Available to members through branch, phone, and online channels

### Mortgages

Available to members and through an **intermediary channel** (dedicated broker portal):

- **Residential mortgages**
- **Products available through Broker Online portal**
- **Intermediary section** of website with criteria, affordability calculator, and contact information

**Key features:**
- Available direct and through intermediaries
- Individual assessment approach
- Intermediary-friendly with dedicated online portal

## Key Differentiators

1. **Historic Buckinghamshire heritage** — Founded 1907 in Chalfont St Giles, over 117 years of service
2. **Aspires to be "best small building society"** — Focused quality ambition rather than growth at all costs
3. **Single-branch model** — All operations from Chalfont St Giles
4. **mybbs online portal** — Digital capability despite small size
5. **Strong intermediary channel** — Dedicated Broker Online portal extends mortgage reach beyond local area
6. **Active CEO community leadership** — Dan Wass personally participates in charity fundraising
7. **Century of high street presence** — Celebrated milestone of 100+ years on the high street
8. **Village/rural community identity** — Based in a Buckinghamshire village, not a city; distinctive character
9. **Financial resilience** — Strengthened capital base in challenging conditions (2024)
10. **Long governance track record** — 118th AGM held in 2025
