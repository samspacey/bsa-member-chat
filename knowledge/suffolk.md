# Suffolk Building Society - Knowledge Base

## Overview

- **Full legal name:** Suffolk Building Society
- **Former names:** Ipswich & Suffolk Freehold Land Society (1849), Ipswich & Suffolk Permanent Benefit Building Society (1886), Ipswich Building Society (1975–2021)
- **Founded:** October 1849, originally as a freehold land society to help ordinary people acquire land and the right to vote
- **Headquarters:** Ransomes Europark, Ipswich, Suffolk (moved from Upper Brook Street in January 2004)
- **Total assets:** ~£655 million (2022, up 7%)
- **Members:** ~65,000+
- **Staff:** ~170 employees
- **CEO:** Richard Norrington (appointed 1 December 2016)
- **Chair:** Peter Elcock
- **CFO:** Paul Johnson
- **COO:** Rebecca Newman
- **Mutual status:** Owned by its saving and borrowing members
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA/PRA regulated:** Yes
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Tagline / positioning:** "A Safe Home for Savers and Safe Homes for our Communities" — Suffolk through and through
- **Name change:** Became Suffolk Building Society on 9 November 2021, with 92.8% member approval, to better reflect the broader community served
- **Identity:** Community-rooted Suffolk mutual with strong social purpose; individual mortgage underwriting; committed to local branches and environmental/social campaigns

## Products

### Savings

Suffolk Building Society offers a comprehensive range of savings products:

- **Easy Access Savings** — Instant access to funds, no restrictions on withdrawals
- **Limited Access Savings** — Higher rates with restrictions on withdrawal frequency
- **Regular Savings** — Monthly deposits to build savings habit
- **Fixed Rate Bonds** — Fixed interest for a set term
- **Cash ISAs** — Tax-free savings
- **Children's Savings** — For young savers
- **Charity Support Accounts** — Savings that contribute to charitable causes
- **Online Accounts** — Open and manage savings digitally
- **Business Savings** — For companies with surplus funds
- **Charity Savings Accounts** — For charitable organisations

**Key features:**
- "No teaser rates" philosophy
- Available in branch, by post, and online
- Members' Lounge for exclusive events and benefits
- Savings interest calculator available on website

### Mortgages

Suffolk Building Society provides mortgages for properties across England and Wales, with individual underwriting:

**Key approach:**
- "We have conversations, not algorithms" — decisions made by experts, not computers
- Individual assessment of each application
- Personal, human approach to lending

**Products available through mortgage finder on website with various filters**

**Key features:**
- Decision in Principle available online (reviewed individually, not instant automated decision)
- Mortgages for properties in England and Wales
- Available direct and through intermediaries
- Specialist/niche lending capabilities
