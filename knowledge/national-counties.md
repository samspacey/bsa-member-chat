- **Staff:** ~196 (2022)

- **Chair:** Simon Wainwright
- **Mutual status:** Independent mutual organisation, owned by its saving and borrowing members; not a subsidiary of any larger group
- **BSA member:** Yes
- **FCA regulated:** Yes (Firm Reference No. 206080)
- **FSCS protected:** Yes
- **Ranking:** 11th largest building society in the UK; UK's largest single-office building society
- **Identity:** Conservative, safety-focused savings and mortgage provider; parent of the Family Building Society

## Branch Network

National Counties Building Society is the **UK's largest single-office building society** — it operates from just one location in Epsom, Surrey:

1. **Principal Office / Head Office** — Ebbisham House, 30 Church Street, Epsom, Surrey KT17 4NL
2. **Counter Services** — Ashley Square, Epsom, Surrey KT18 5DD

All business nationwide is conducted from Epsom — by post, phone, and online. This single-office model keeps overheads low and is a key part of the society's identity.

## Products

### Savings

Since approximately 2016, new savings accounts are offered primarily through the **Family Building Society** brand (a subsidiary launched in 2014). Existing NCBS savers continue to be served under the National Counties name.

**Account types (via Family Building Society):**
- **Easy Access Accounts** — Variable rate, instant access
- **Bonds** — Fixed rate savings for set terms
- **ISAs** — Tax-free savings with different access options (easy access, fixed rate)
- **Children's Accounts** — Savings for under-18s ("Bank of Mum and Dad")
- **Notice Accounts** — Higher rates in exchange for advance notice of withdrawals

Existing NCBS savings bonds and Fixed Rate Cash ISAs approaching maturity are offered reinvestment options including Family Building Society accounts.

### Mortgages

Since 1 December 2016, all new National Counties mortgages are offered exclusively under the **Family Building Society** brand.

**Key mortgage features:**
- Individual case-by-case underwriting by a dedicated team
- Conservative lending approach resulting in very low mortgage arrears
- Products designed for different customer needs
- Available through intermediaries

**Family Building Society mortgage products include:**
- Residential mortgages
- Products designed to help families (the society's founding purpose)
- Intergenerational mortgage support

**Existing NCBS mortgage borrowers** continue to be managed under the National Counties name with no changes required.

### Family Building Society (Subsidiary)

In July 2014, National Counties launched the **Family Building Society** — the first new building society created in the UK since the Ecology Building Society in 1981. It was specifically designed to:
- Help all generations of families with financial challenges
- Offer a wider set of financial solutions than a traditional building society
- Tackle intergenerational housing and savings problems

The Family Building Society now handles all new savings and mortgage products for the NCBS Group.

2. **Family Building Society subsidiary** — First new building society in the UK in over 30 years; innovative approach to intergenerational finance
3. **Conservative lending philosophy** — Individual underwriting, very low arrears, strong capital position
4. **Financial strength** — CET1 ratio of 19.1% and reserves of £171.3 million provide robust safety
5. **Independence** — One of the largest fully independent building societies in the UK
6. **Over 50,000 customers** served from a single office — demonstrates efficiency of centralised model
7. **Dual-brand strategy** — NCBS for existing relationships, Family BS for new products and innovation

## Strengths

- Exceptional financial strength and capital position
- Very conservative, low-risk lending approach
- UK's largest single-office building society — efficient operating model
- Innovative Family Building Society subsidiary
- Strong independence
- Over 125 years of continuous operation
- Dedicated underwriting team for individual case assessment

## Weaknesses

- Single office location — no branch network for face-to-face service outside Epsom
- Dual-brand strategy (NCBS/Family BS) may be confusing for some members
- New products only available under Family BS brand — legacy NCBS members may feel the parent brand is being wound down
- Conservative approach means fewer niche/specialist products
- Limited public profile despite significant size
- Website could be more modern and user-friendly

## Member Demographics

- **Primary members:** Savers across England and Wales seeking safety and competitive rates
- **Geographic spread:** Nationwide, managed from Epsom, Surrey
- **Typical savers:** Risk-averse individuals attracted by strong capital position and mutual status
- **Mortgage borrowers:** Now primarily through Family Building Society brand; focus on families and intergenerational lending
- **Local presence:** Strong local identity in Epsom and Surrey
